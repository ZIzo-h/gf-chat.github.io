Our Courses

Camera Operations

Students are taught on the use and operations of camera for photography and visual storytelling in a motion picture or televison show.

Editing

The student will learn the techniques used to manipulate and arrange video shots using video editing software. The student will learn trimming, sequencing clips, adding transition and other special effects to bring about a quality, compelling and attractive video as the output.

Sound engineering

The student will learn how to be a location sound recordist or a location sound engineer as a member of a movie/television production crew. He/she will be taught how to use professional adio equipment to record/edit sound, for later inclusion in the finished product using relevant softeware, or for reference.

Acting

In this course, the student will learn how to enact as an actor or actress who adopts a charcter-in theatre,television,film,radio or any other medium that makes use of the mimetic mode. The student's skill (a well-developed imagination, emotional facility, physical expressivity, vocal projection, clarity of speech and the ability to interpret drama) are developed etc.

Lighting

Here, the student is taught the importance of the proper amount of illumination and lighting effects required for different scenes in any televion production.

Script Writing

This course teaches students on how to translate their stories and movie ideas into an interesting, compelling and marketable script.

Directing

This course aims to teach the student the art of leading dramatic performances on the stage or in films. The student learns the different roles of a director in bringing out the best performance from the actors.

Special Effects Makeup

Students are trained on the process of using prosthetic sculpting, molding and casting techniques to create advanced cosmetic effects.

Movie Production

In this course, the student will learn the basic phases employed in every televison production, be it a sitcom episode, a feature-lenghth movie, a corporate training video, an educational program, or a newscast. This will help him/her to efficiently create and deliver an excellent content to the audience.
